A quick-start resource for new staff, graduate students, and faculty. Find what you need, know who to ask, and get oriented fast.

About the Design Lab

Who we are

An academic unit within the University of California San Diego, with 8 staff, 58 faculty members, graduate students, and undergrads. The Design Lab brings together researchers, educators, and practitioners to advance the field of design.

What's new

We've launched a new Master in Design program, bringing a wave of new students, staff, and faculty into the lab. This guide is here to help you find your footing quickly.
